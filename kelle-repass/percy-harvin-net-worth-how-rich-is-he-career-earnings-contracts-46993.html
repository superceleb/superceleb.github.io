Percy Harvin Net Worth | How Rich Is He | Career Earnings &amp; Contracts

Publish date: 2024-05-01

Percy Harvin is a retired professional football player from the United States. During his NFL career, he spent eight seasons with four different teams, earning $42.21 million.

In the first round of the 2009 NFL Draft, he was chosen by the Minnesota Vikings. During his career, Harvin also played for the Seattle, New York Jets, and Buffalo Bills. In his first season with the Seahawks, Harvin was vital in their Super Bowl victory over the Broncos.

Following that, Seattle traded the wide receiver to the Jets in November 2014. Harvin signed a one-year agreement with the Bills in 2015 after a brief run with the Jets. He returned to the field for one more season in 2016, handicapped by injuries, before retiring from professional football.

Percy Harvin Earnings, Contracts, and Net Worth
