Biography

Luz Elena González was born in Guadalajara, Mexico on August 22, 1974. Model turned telenovela star who has become most widely known for her recurring roles in Hasta que el dinero nos separe, Libre para amarte, and La escuelita VIP. She is also a successful presenter, working for Siempre en domingo and Al ritmo de la noche, amongst others. Her cousin, Susana Gonzalez, is also a well known telenovela star. She first rose to fame in 1994 as the winner of Nuestra Belleza Jalisco. It was with this title that she earned her spot in the Nuestra Belleza México competition.
