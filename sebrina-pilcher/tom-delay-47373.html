Biography

Tom DeLay was born in Texas on April 8, 1947. United States Congressman who represented Texas’ 22nd district from 1984 until 2006 and served as House Majority Leader from 2003 to 2005. His memoir – No Retreat, No Surrender: One American’s Fight – was published in 2007. He helped Newt Gingrich start the K Street Project, an effort to advance Republican ideals. He graduated from the University of Houston in 1970 with a Bachelor of Science degree in biology; he obtained student deferments to avoid service in the Vietnam War.
