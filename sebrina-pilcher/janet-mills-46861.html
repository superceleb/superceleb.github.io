Biography

Janet Mills was born in Maine on December 30, 1947. After serving in the Maine Legislature, this Democratic Party politician became Attorney General of Maine. During her second term as Attorney General of Maine, she served under the gubernatorial administration of Paul LePage. She studied at both the University of Massachusetts, Boston and the University of Maine, Portland.
