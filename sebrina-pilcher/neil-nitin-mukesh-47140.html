Biography

Neil Nitin Mukesh was born in India on January 15, 1982. Famous for his International Indian Film Academy Award-winning work in the 2007 Bollywood thriller Johnny Gaddaar, Mukesh is also notable for his Filmfare Award-nominated performance in New York (2009), as well as for his Screen Award-nominated portrayal of Major Edwin Rodriques in 7 Khoon Maaf (2011). He and Ameesha Patel co-star in the 2013 feature Shortcut Romeo. He made childhood appearances in the feature films Vijay (1988) and Jaisi Karni Waisi Bharni (1989). Before continuing his acting career as an , he earned a business degree from HR College-Mumbai.
