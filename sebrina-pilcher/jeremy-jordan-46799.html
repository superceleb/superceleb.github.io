Biography

Jeremy Jordan was born in Corpus Christi, TX on November 20, 1984. Broadway and television actor who played the role of Clyde Barrow in the 2011 musical version of Bonnie & Clyde and Jack Kelly in the 2012 musical Newsies. He appeared on 17 episodes of the NBC series Smash in 2013 and 2015, he was cast on the CBS series Supergirl. He was on an episode of the television series Law & Order with Richard Belzer. Raised in a low-income household but a “straight-A student,” he later attended and graduated from Ithaca College earning a BFA in theater.
