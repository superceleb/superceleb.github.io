Omer Fedi- Biography

Omer Fedi is an Israeli guitarist, songwriter, and producer located in Tel Aviv. He co-wrote, produced, and performed on “Mood” by 24kGoldn and Iann Dior, “Montero (Call Me by Your Name)” by Lil Nas X, and “Stay” by Kid Laroi and Justin Bieber. These songs all peaked at the top of the Billboard Hot 100. He is one of the “top hip hop producers of 2020,” according to XXL magazine.

What is the Net Worth of Omer Fedi? Salary, Earnings

Omer Fedi’s net worth is believed to be $3 million. He makes the most money from playing guitar, producing, and co-writing. This statistic includes only his earnings from songs, royalties, and personal products.

Omer Fedi- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings

Omer Fedi was born in Tel Aviv, Gush Dan, Israel on March 25, 2000. His father, Asher Fedi, is a very competent and well-respected drummer in Israel. His father trained him to play the drums from a young age, but at the age of 10, he turned to guitar after being inspired by Drake Bell’s character in Drake & Josh. Fedi and his father moved to Los Angeles when he was 16 to seek a career in music. There is no information known about his mother.

Omer Fedi attended Los Angeles’ Calabasas High School. When he joined his high school’s jazz band and his concerts drew crowds from outside the traditional parent audience, he became famous. In 2018, he competed in the Reno Jazz Festival and received the title of “Outstanding Performer,” beating out over 9,000 other contenders. Sam Hook, a songwriter, approached him the following year and invited him to collaborate on a project.

Omer Fedi- Professional Career

Omer Fedi began working as a professional guitarist in 2018. His first composition was Ella Mai, a song by Ella Mai (singer). Fedi debuted on Machine Gun Kelly’s COVID-19 lockdown session in March 2020, covering songs such as Misery Business and Waiting for the World to Change. Fedi collaborated on the writing, production, and vocals for 24kGoldn’s “City of Angels,” which will be published on March 31, 2020. Go! by Kid Laroi, which he co-produced with Juice Wrld, was released on June 12th. He produced and composed Iann Dior’s I’m Gone, which was released on the same day. Fedi co-wrote and sung Mood by 24kGoldn with Iann Dior, which was published on July 24, 2020, and peaked at number one for 33 weeks.

Fedi produced and co-wrote Kid Laroi’s F*ck Love mixtape, which was released on the same day. Omer co-wrote, composed, and produced “Without You” with Kid Laroi’s “F*ck Love” deluxe, F*ck Love Savage, which was released on November 6th. The song peaked at number 34 on the Billboard Hot 100. Fedi performed and produced Lil Nas X’s song “Montero (Call Me by Your Name),” which was released on March 26, 2021, on the same day as 24kGoldn’s El Dorado album, which Fedi also performed and produced. Fedi co-wrote and co-produced Sun Goes Down, the second song on Lil Nas X’s album Montero, which was released on May 21, 2021. On July 9, 2021, he produced and released the track “Stay” by Kid Laroi and Justin Bieber.
