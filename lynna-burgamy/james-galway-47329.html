Biography

James Galway was born in Belfast, Northern Ireland on December 8, 1939. Irish flute player of both classical and popular music who has released more than forty albums since 1979. He followed the footsteps of Jean-Pierre Rampal and became one of the first flute players to establish an international career as a soloist. After he moved to London as a teenager to pursue a career as a flautist, he studied music at the Paris Conservatoire.
