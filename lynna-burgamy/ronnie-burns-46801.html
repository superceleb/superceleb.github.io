Biography

Ronnie Burns was born in Illinois on July 9, 1935. Best known as the adopted son of famous comedians George Burns and Gracie Allen, he appeared during the 1950s in both The Burns & Allen Show and The George Burns Show. He also briefly starred in NBC’s early 1960s series, Happy. He appeared with actor-turned-television writer and producer Burt Metcalfe in NBC’s Happy. A native of Evanston, Illinois, he was adopted by Burns and Allen when he was just two months old. His slightly older sister, Sandra Jean, was also adopted.
