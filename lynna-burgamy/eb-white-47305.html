Biography

EB White was born in Mount Vernon, NY on July 11, 1899. American author who penned the children’s classics Charlotte’s Web and Stuart Little. He is also known for his contributions to the English-language writing guide The Elements of Style. William Strunk, Jr., a former teacher of White, wrote the original Elements of Style, which White would later expand. He worked as a reporter for the United Press and the Seattle Times after graduating from Cornell University in 1921. Later, he joined the staff of The New Yorker magazine and was a contributor to the publication for nearly six decades.
