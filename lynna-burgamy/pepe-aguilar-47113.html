Who exactly is Pepe Aguilar?

Pepe Aguilar is a Mexican-American actor and singer-songwriter. Equinoccio Records is his own record label. He also owns the most technologically advanced studio in Latin America. Pepe has four Grammys and four Latin Grammys to his name. He is the son of singer-actors Antonio Aguilar and Flor Silvestre from Mexico.

Pepe Aguilar’s Professional Life and Career

Pepe made his singing debut when he was three years old. His first singing job came when his father, Antonio Aguilar, performed at Madison Square Garden in New York. Aguilar was a devotee of rock music as a teenager and was a member of the band “Equs.”
