Biography

Peewee Longway was born in Atlanta, GA on August 17, 1984. Signed by 1017 Records, this rapper’s most notable mixtape became The Blue M&M, featuring the singles “Sneakin n Geekin” and “Servin Lean.” He released his debut studio album Mr. Blue Benjamin in January of 2016. His uncle Cold Blooded Da Don introduced him to Gucci Mane which led to his signing with 1017 Records. He, Gucci, and Young Dolph formed the rap trio Felix Brothers. His debut mixtape Money, Pounds, Ammunition was released in March of 2013. He put out two more mixtapes that same year called Running Around The Lobby and Lobby Runners.
