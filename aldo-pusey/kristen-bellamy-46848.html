Kristen Bellamy’s Professional Career

Kristen began her career in the entertainment sector as Lydia in the 1997 film “Born into Exile.” The same year, she played a college girl in the television drama “House of Frankenstein.” Over the years, she has played minor roles such as a flight attendant in the 2003 film “Fastlane.” She had an appearance on the show “Rachel Rey” in 2013. She then portrayed Sally in the 2016 film “The Bounce Back.” She also starred in the show “Dinner at Tiffani’s” in 2017. Her most recent part was in “The Fix” in 2019.
