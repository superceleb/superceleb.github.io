Sarah Snyder- Biography

Sarah Snyder is a model, actress, stylist, and social media celebrity. Snyder first made news in 2015 when she began dating Jaden Smith, the son of famed Hollywood actor and musician Will Smith. Snyder’s career took off after their two-year public engagement ended, and she went on to become an Instagram sensation, a sought-after model, and an aspiring actress. Her professional career began in 2008.

Sarah Snyder- Relationship, Boyfriend

Sarah Snyder made headlines in 2015 when it was revealed that she was dating another aspiring star, Jaden Smith, the son of Hollywood royalty. From the start, the two were open about their relationship, frequently appearing in public together and sharing photos on their social media accounts. As previously stated, the exposure generated by their relationship aided Sarah’s already flourishing career. The media followed Jaden and Sarah everywhere they went, but they didn’t let the attention affect their relationship. They were together for two years until 2017, and neither star disclosed the specific reasons for their split, but according to reports, they remained excellent friends and still keep in touch.

Sarah has collaborated with “Dolce & Gabbana,” “Yeezy,” “Hugo Boss,” “Uniqlo,” and a slew of other high-end brands since her meteoric climb to fame. She created her own distinct clothing style by combining expensive, fashionable items with loose sweatshirts, jogging pants, and other “athleisure” clothing items. She is now represented by the modeling agencies “REMM,” “Elite Paris,” “Elite Milan,” and “Modelwerk.”

Sarah Snyder- Professional Career

Sarah’s initial passion was styling and working with garments. She had never considered modeling as a possible professional path for herself, but once she moved to New York, she eagerly grabbed any opportunity that came her way. She gradually established a name for herself in the fashion business, drawing some of the industry’s greatest names. Her friendship with Jaden Smith only aided her in gaining greater fame and, as a result, more modeling contracts.

Snyder began her career in the acting field after a successful career in fashion and modeling. She landed her first role in the 2008 film “Skeleton Key 2: 667 Neighbor of the Beast,” and after moving to New York and beginning her modeling career, she was cast in several others, including the 2010 film “Scarlet Rain,” in which she played Dotty, the 2012 film “Greencastle,” and the 2013 horror film “Dead Woman’s Hollow.”

Sarah was seen having dinner with Leonardo DiCaprio in November 2017, sparking speculations of another connection with a prominent actor.
