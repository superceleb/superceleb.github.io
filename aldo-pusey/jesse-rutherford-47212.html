Biography

Jesse Rutherford was born in Thousand Oaks, CA on August 21, 1991. Lead singer for the California indie band The Neighbourhood. Their hit single “Sweater Weather” reached number one on Billboard’s alternative chart in June 2013. He is joined in The Neighbourhood by guitarists Jeremy Freedman and Zach Abels, bassist Mikey Margott and drummer Brandon Fried. He impersonated Elvis Presley and NSYNC in talent shows. He acted when he was younger, playing Daniel on The Young and the Restless.
