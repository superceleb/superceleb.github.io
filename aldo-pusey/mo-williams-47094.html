Biography

Mo Williams was born in Jackson, MS on December 19, 1982. Sharpshooting NBA point guard who played for such teams as the Milwaukee Bucks, Utah Jazz, Cleveland Cavaliers, Portland Trail Blazers, and Minnesota Timberwolves. He helped the Cavaliers win the NBA Championship in 2016. He was teammates with LeBron James on the Cavaliers from 2008 to 2011 and again from 2015-2017. He played two seasons at the University of Alabama before entering the NBA Draft in 2003.
