Biography

Sachin Pilgaonkar was born in India on August 17, 1957. Actor, director, and singer who starred as Ahmed in the 1975 action-adventure Hindi film, Sholay. His other credits include Navri Mile Navryala, Amchyasarkhe Aamich, and Ashi Hi Banwa Banwi. Both he and actor Mohanlal Nair have won National Film Awards. He began his career as a child actor, winning two National Film Awards for Best Child Artiste – first in 1962, then in 1972.
