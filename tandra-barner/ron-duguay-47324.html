Ron Duguay – Biography

Ron Duguay was born in Canada on July 6, 1957. NHL center who spent the bulk of his 12-year career with the New York Rangers. He recorded 274 goals and 346 assists across his career. Ron was in the 1981-82 NHL All-Star Game, along with another great center from Ontario, Canada, Wayne Gretzky. He was drafted 13th overall by the New York Rangers in 1977.
