Biography

Angelo Torres was born in Puerto Rico on April 14, 1932. American cartoonist and caricaturist whose work has been featured in many comic books, including a long-running regular slot in Mad magazine. He and Sergio Aragones both worked for Mad Magazine. He occasionally worked for EC Comics during the 1950s.
