Biography

Torrey Smith was born in Colonial Beach, VA on January 26, 1989. Speedy NFL wide receiver who helped the Baltimore Ravens win Super Bowl XLVII in 2013. He had his breakout campaign in 2013, where he topped 1,000 yards for the first time in his career. After his time in Baltimore, he spent two years with the San Francisco 49ers, then signed with the Philadelphia Eagles in 2017. He won Super Bowl LII as a member of the Eagles in 2018. He and Anquan Boldin made up a very explosive wide receiver core for Joe Flacco and the Ravens. He was a dominant All-ACC wide receiver while attending the University of Maryland.
